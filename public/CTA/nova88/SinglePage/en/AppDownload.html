iOS
Follow these steps and get your own Nova88 Mobile App now!
  • Tap Download Now and install.
  • Go to Settings > General > Device Management.
  • Select the Developer name.
  • Tap on the Developer name and then tap “Trust”.

Android
Follow these steps and get your own Nova88 Mobile App now!
  • Click the Download Now button and select install.
  • Go to Settings > Security and turn on Unknown Sources.
  • Download Nova88 Mobile App APK.
  • Go to File Manager > Downloads and run the apk file to begin the installation.
  • Open the app, enter your username & password & enjoy.
